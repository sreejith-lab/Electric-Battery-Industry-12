The electric battery industry is a rapidly growing global sector, driven primarily by the increasing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and the shift towards renewable energy. This growth is fueled by supportive government policies, declining battery costs, and technological advancements in areas like lithium-ion batteries.

The global market for electric batteries has grown exponentially over the last decade, driven primarily by the rise of electric mobility and renewable energy integration. According to recent estimates, the global battery market is expected to exceed $200 billion by 2030, with lithium-ion batteries dominating the landscape. Governments, industries, and consumers are increasingly adopting batteries as part of efforts to decarbonize energy systems and reduce dependence on fossil fuels.
