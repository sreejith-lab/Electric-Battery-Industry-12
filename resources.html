Primary Raw Materials

1

Lithium

A fundamental component of lithium-ion batteries, critical for energy storage.

2

Nickel

Used extensively in nickel-manganese-cobalt (NMC) cathodes, contributing to higher energy density and longer lifecycles.

3

Cobalt

Essential for the performance and lifespan of NMC batteries.

4

Manganese

Helps stabilize NMC cathodes, improving overall battery performance.

5

Graphite

A primary material for the anode, which stores lithium ions.

6

Silicon

An emerging material for anodes that can increase energy density .

7

Copper

Forms the conductive backbone for the anode.

8

Aluminium

Used as the current collector for the cathode.
